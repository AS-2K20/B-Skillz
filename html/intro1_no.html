Here are some facts from Wikipedia...

'Sherlock Holmes' is a fictional private detective created by British author Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.

Referring to himself as a "consulting detective" in the stories, Holmes is known for his proficiency with observation, deduction, forensic science, and logical reasoning.

Though not the first fictional detective, Sherlock Holmes is arguably the best known.

GUINESS WORLD RECORDS lists him as the most portrayed literary human character in film and television history.
